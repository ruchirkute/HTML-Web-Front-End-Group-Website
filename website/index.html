Project Description

This website is developed by a team of six members namely Vidit Mody (Team Leader), Shruti Patel, Ruchir Kute, Brijesh Patel, Janak Patel and Hardik Rupareliya. It highlights the use of techologies such as Node.js, Javascript, Geolocation, Socket.io, PHP, HTML5 and CSS3. Our website is for online gaming and entertainment. People can play games here, chat with us and even can send emails. People have to register first in order to play games. Once they are registered, they can sign in with their username and password to enjoy games. They can also chat with us online with an additional feature of sending a mail to us in case of any problem to our website.

Take a look at our website features such as Geolocation, Online Gaming and Chat Online.
